About Us

Hi! My name is Sabina, I am a florist with 25 years of experience. I connect my passion with a profession, what gives me real satisfaction. As far as I can remember I have seen an inspiration and huge potential in the flowers. I have started my adventure by opening a local flower shop. As I was hungry for more, my next step was starting a decoration company, with the specialization in weddings. I love to work dynamically, to jump into new ideas.. and that is my newest project - unique flower composition.
